Hello! I am a first-year PhD student in the CSE department at UC San Diego in the [theory group](https://cstheory.ucsd.edu/home.html). I am very fortunate to be advised by Professors [Sanjoy Dasgupta](https://cseweb.ucsd.edu/~dasgupta/) and [Mikhail Belkin](http://misha.belkin-wang.org/). Prior to my PhD, I completed a master's in computer science at Columbia, where I researched under the excellent mentorship of Professor [Alexandr Andoni](http://www.cs.columbia.edu/~andoni/). I also spent the summer before my PhD as an ML Research intern with the Core Machine Learning group at [Goldman Sachs](https://www.goldmansachs.com/careers/divisions/engineering/). My undergraduate degree is in mathematics with a minor in chemistry from UChicago. 

Broadly, I am passionate about using theory to design computational methods that are powerful and practical. Specifically, I have focused on the development of practical algorithms with performance and correctness guarantees for machine learning and game theory.

Feel free to email me: dbeaglehole {at} ucsd {dot} edu

I like brazilian jiu jitsu, mexican food, and guitar :)
